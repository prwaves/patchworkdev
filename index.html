  • Responsive Design Layouts

    John Langford Realty - www.johnlangfordrealty.com

    John Langford Realty provided me with a design that needed to be implemented into a fluid layout. This means that the content automatically adjusts depending on the size of the screen displaying the website. Essentially, the same code provides a usable layout no matter what device you are browsing with: a smartphone, a tablet, or a desktop computer.

    To complete this project, I used HTML 5, CSS 3, jQuery, and PHP.

  • Wordpress Plugins

    SeatGeek - www.seatgeek.com/blog

    SeatGeek needed a way to enforce their SEO guidelines and track how closely their writers are abiding by them.   • Custom Web Apps

    My Audio Bio - www.myaudiobio.com

    My Audio Bio is a web service that serves as a way to share the stories and the songs that make up one's history. My Audio Bio serves as an online autobiographical catalogue of your musical history.

    I was tasked with all of the development involved in taking My Audio Bio from design to completion. (Design provided by Nick Hollomon). This included PSD to HTML conversion, implementation of a Wordpress blog, and even the design and development of a SOAP web service that was used by the iPhone app version of MyAudioBio.

    To complete this project, I used Zend Framework, Wordpress, HTML, CSS, jQuery, and PHP.

  • Content Management Systems

The chosen CMS Platform was Concrete5, which allows the client to choose from a set of pre-configured templates and easily add new pages and subpages.
